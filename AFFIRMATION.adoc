=== The central geometric result stands

A glider cannot cleanly cross the settled period-30 stream at *any* of the 30
launch phases. Measured with true cell-overlap and *no proximity rule at all*
(`verify8.mjs`): 21 of 30 phases overlap outright, the remaining 9 reach exactly
Chebyshev 1 — adjacency that annihilates both patterns on the next generation —
and *0 of 30* reach distance 2 or more.

The cause is *geometry, not phase*: stream gliders sit roughly 15 units apart
and a perpendicular crosser needs roughly 17. No launch timing can help.

== What we do NOT affirm

Stated because an affirmation that only lists successes is marketing.

No formal proof.:: Nothing here is mechanically proved. Verification is
exhaustive empirical sweep over all 30 phases plus an argued spacing lemma. That
is a real and strong standard; it is not proof, and this repository declares no
`formal-proofs` capability.

The no-go lemma's generality is argued, not swept.:: The 0/30 result is
exhaustive for *perpendicular diagonal* crossings of a settled p30 stream.
Beyond that geometry it rests on the spacing arithmetic, which is prose.

"Unsolvable by feel" is a design judgement.:: The standing falsifier — that the
level must not be learnable without reading phase — is not mechanised. No CI job
checks it. An automated solver that could certify it is recorded in
`Intentfile.a2ml` as the highest-value future work.

Playability is not automatically tested.:: The witness route is verified
headless at all 30 launch ticks, and `build.mjs` re-proves the bundle
completable. Nothing tests the browser UI, input handling, or rendering.

One claim has already died here.:: An earlier sweep reported a second clean
landing lane. It was false — an artefact of `stamp()` silently absorbing
out-of-bounds writes. `stamp()` now throws and the corrected sweep finds one
lane. The retraction is recorded in `VERIFICATION.md` and `CHANGELOG.md` rather
than quietly removed, and it is the reason this project treats a crashing script
as a bug and a completed negative result as a finding.

  internally calls `actions/upload-artifact` by tag, and this organisation

  a Dependabot merge. Corrected — the comment is the only human-readable part of
