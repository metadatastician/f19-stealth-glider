### Changed

- `VERIFICATION.md` claim #3 now cites `src/verify3.mjs`. It previously pointed
  at a session transcript, which is not a runnable artefact.

### Deliberately omitted

Recorded here because absence is otherwise indistinguishable from oversight;
full rationale in [`AUDIT.adoc`](AUDIT.adoc).

- **`mirror.yml`, `instant-sync.yml`, `push-email-notify.yml`** — the
  notification-storm workflows frozen across the organisation. Re-committing
  them would re-arm an amplifier that was just disabled.
- **An advanced `codeql.yml`** — GitHub code-scanning *default setup* is already
  enabled for this repository. An advanced workflow conflicts with it and fails
  permanently. Scanning is present; the workflow file is not.
- **`.ncl` / `.k9.ncl` contractile runners** — not built anywhere in the estate.
  Shipping them would be scaffolding that cannot execute, which is precisely the
  false-green pattern the contractiles exist to catch.

## [0.1.0] — 2026-07-20

First public release. Operation Nightstep v0.

### Added

- **B3/S23 engine** (`src/engine.mjs`) — bounded grid, RLE parsing, the eight
  square symmetries, bounds-checked `stamp`, a named pattern library including
  the Gosper glider gun, empirically derived glider kinematics, and
  shadow-filtered glider reacquisition.
- **Mission layer** (`src/mission.mjs`) — Chebyshev-2 contact rule, sensors,
  trace accounting frozen at the hangar threshold, hangar splice, ambient
  stepping after mission end.
- **Operation Nightstep v2 level** (`src/level.mjs`) — 172×108, spawn `[8,16]`,
  hangar `[86,94]`, fence row at `y=54` with a single breach, Gosper gun firing
  a period-30 stream down lane 14 as the impassable eastern border.
- **Verification ledger** — 11 claims in `VERIFICATION.md`, each discharged by a
  named script runnable with plain `node`.
- **Self-contained playable bundle** — `f19-stealth-glider.html`, generated by
  `src/build.mjs`, which replays the witness on the transformed core so the
  shipped artefact is verified as shipped.
- **Real CI** — the cited ledger (`verify1,2,3,8,9` + `build`), a licence-header
  check, and a byte-for-byte bundle-reproducibility assertion.

### Resolved

- **Is a period-30 glider stream crossable at all under this geometry?**
  **No — 0 of 30 clean crossings, at every launch phase.** Under a true
  cell-overlap rule with no proximity radius, 21 of 30 phases overlap outright
  and the remaining 9 pass only at Chebyshev 1, an adjacency that annihilates on
  the next generation.

  The cause is **geometry, not phase**: glider spacing in the stream is about 15
  units, while a perpendicular crosser needs roughly 17. No phase gap is ever
  wide enough, so no launch timing can help. The level therefore routes *around*
  the stream through a phase-independent fence breach, and the witness lands at
  all 30 launch ticks with zero trace.

### Fixed

- `src/verify3.mjs` and `src/verify9.mjs` crashed with `stamp out of bounds` at
  the most negative approach lanes. Both sweeps now enlarge the grid and
  co-shift glider and eater together, which preserves the measured relative lane
  because Life is translation-invariant away from borders. The fix is in the
  harness, not in `stamp` — see below.
- CI `startup_failure` at 0s: the organisation enforces `sha_pinning_required`,
  and the workflow used tag refs. Every action is now pinned to a full commit
  SHA, and `actions/setup-node` was dropped entirely since `ubuntu-latest` ships
  Node 20.

### Retracted

- An earlier claim of a **second clean landing lane** was false. It was produced
  by `stamp` silently absorbing out-of-bounds writes. `stamp` now throws, and
  the corrected sweep shows exactly one clean lane (λ = 0). The claim died in
  testing and is recorded rather than quietly removed.
